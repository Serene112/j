About

J2G Click & Smile is a photo booth service business operating in the events and celebrations industry. The business provides interactive and memorable photo booth experiences through high-quality printed outputs such as standee photos, photo strips, and magnetic prints. J2G Click & Smile is unique in its affordable pricing, flexible service options, and efficient in-house operation. As part of an existing resort business, the photo booth service is readily available for resort events while also being offered as a standalone service for external bookings. This setup allows the business to minimize operational costs while maintaining consistent service quality.

THE BOOTH

J2G Click & Smile Photobooth caters birthdays, weddings, corporate events, and private celebrations.
